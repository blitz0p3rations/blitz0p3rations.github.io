## 1 day in after county jail, how to act ?

The first day is pure recongnization and study phase of others, 
it is a mental battle that others are already ready to face, the thing to do is to act like normal people,
deny all questions related to addictions or crimes, it is likely that the first few months
your behavior will be studied by the guards so as to place you in the appropriate sector, 
beware of fakes who try to get moved by sector to predate against normal people

## How to deal prisoners test?
the first few days some groups of prisoners usually divided by race will try to get you to fight one of them, 
respect is the only thing that matters in there and you have to fight pre prove to them that you have courage, 
it's not about fighting skills but about the rules of the road, fight for honor and respect, 
if you don't you will be fodder for everyone and dig your own grave
