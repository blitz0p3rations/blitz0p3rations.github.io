## Hospital Account takeovers

During an assessment on one of the major hospitals in the state where I live, 
from simple web apps exposed externally it was possible through contentndisocvery operations 
to find open a debug page containing the session cookies of all users logged into the application.
In a short time, taking advantage of those valid cookies, I logged in like many people and with a
large variety of privileges they execute contracts, databases of medicines and sometimes being able to interact
with the machinery, the impact is critical considering the environment but
I am as always amazed by how easy it is to do this, even one who is just starting out could have done 
it and fortunately that the vulnerability has not been exploited by malicious actors.
