## Review
The material to study is not updated and in the real world you will need little,
but for a beginner it can still be interesting.
The exam lasts a week and you have all the time in the world, 
simulate a real pentest with double pivoting to reach a DMZ, web vulnerabilities are
elementary and taking the first shell is really easy, from there you just need to go over
some labs of the course and exploits easily other machines, the BoF can be insidious for those 
who have never faced it but in the end it is vanilla and linear, considering that it is 90% metasploit
the exam is only the ability to manage a somewhat complex PT, for beginners it can be stimulating and nice to deal with, 
I only suggest it to them for others better to tack on something else.
