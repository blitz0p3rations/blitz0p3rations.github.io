## Ultras and Hooligans mindset, the new approach

Disclaimair I am not an ultras, I only partially share their mentality and I deny 
any type of verbal and physical violence, I go to the sectors where the ultras have been present 
for years and I have learned and acknowledged their way of thinking and their mentality which makes 
me it allows you to anticipate and predict the moves they make in the panorama, like me there are 
thousands who are not part of ultras groups but who love to go to the sectors where they are present, 
to better enjoy the matches and find a place in the world.

First of all, the ultras are right-wing and the hoolingas are left-wing,
They act out of an extreme romanticism towards a faith, 
where they draw the burden of fighting with their rivals, 
football is of little interest only for glory and status, 
in their life everything changes, women, work but faith remains 
and fighting in the name of that faith represents for them the maximum expression of love.

In the old school thinking that is slowly disappearing
we made an appointment to fight loyally with the rules of the ultras world made 
of improvised weapons and ethics in fighting, with the last groups of Eastern Europe the
mentality is changing in favor of sudden sunny just to dominate the opponent, look at Serbia where many 
groups are sponsored by the state and do their dirty work by eliminating political opponents.

The old look does not share the strategies of the new movements, but either they adapt or they 
are destined to be overwhelmed by the new, more violent and unethical groups, unfortunately in 
the future they will have more and more space and even the last crumb of their romanticism will vanish.
