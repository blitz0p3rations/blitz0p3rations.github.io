These readings have been done only and exclusively to learn how to act in 
situations where the only possible way is to fight, 
with no possibility of escape, they are just to have them in my cultural baggage. {: .lead} <!–-break-–> 

## 21 Techniques of silent killing

Clearly I will only speak in general and without going into details, 
the books are available to everyone, it was half a scam from my point of view, in 
fact it only explains 3 techniques and a whole series of variations to be adopted based on 
the position of the target, useful only to have a vague idea of 
how to act and what factors to take into consideration.

## Killing Without Joy

Again without going into details, a very best and complete book 
that explains a vast variety of arsenal and techniques, it also takes into consideration
the best solution based on the target and its position, I expected a more accurate 
description on the practical part but I think can be considered satisfactory.
